How I can help

Let me be your guide to identify your needs and define the steps to reach your website's goals.

Whether you need a new website, a redesign or maintenance of your existing site, together we will create intuitive and easy to navigate interfaces that are a perfect fit for your project and values.

With responsive design, your website will look great on devices big and small. And by focussing on reducing your website’s ecological footprint, your site will be more sustainable and accessible.

Website Development

  • Easy navigation with intuitive and responsive design
  • Website in Wordpress or built from scratch
  • Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)
  • Instructions for updating content yourself (if you wish to do so!)

Website Maintainance

  • Creating new sections of the website
  • Adjustments on layout and branding
  • New functionality
  • Improvement in accessibility, findability and speed
  • Translation functionality to make your website multilingual

Support for online courses

  • Customization and branding of your Learning Management System (Moodle, Thinkific and other LMS.)
  • Adjustment of lessons and learning materials for your MOOCs and e-courses
  • Creation of templates and guidance
