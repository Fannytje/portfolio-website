How I can help

Whether you need a new website, a redesign or maintenance of your existing site, I'll guide you through to successfully define your needs and the steps needed to reach your goals.

Together we will create intuitive and easy to navigate interfaces that are a perfect fit for your project and values.

By focussing on accessibility and reducing your website’s ecological footprint, it will rank high in search engines results so your clients can quickly find the exact information they need.

With responsive design your website will look great on devices big and small.

Website Development

  • Easy navigation with intuitive and responsive design
  • Website in Wordpress or built from scratch
  • Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)
  • Guidance to create engaging texts and images for your audience
  • Instructions for updating content yourself (if you wish to do so!)

Website Maintainance

  • Creating new sections of the website
  • Adjustments on layout and branding
  • New functionality
  • Improvement in accessibility, findability and speed
  • Adding translation functionality to make your website multilingual

Support for online courses

  • Customization and branding of your Learning Management System (Moodle, Thinkific and other LMS.)
  • Adjustment of lessons and learning materials for your MOOCs and e-courses
  • Creation of templates and guidance
